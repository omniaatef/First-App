Welcome to Online Poll

It's an application which helps you to connect with your friends for doing an activity

About this application

This is a great place to share your activity with your friends. This application features:

  • You can suggest an activity and share it with your friends
  • You can define the type of this activity (eating, praying, or outing)
  • You can see your friends' votes and decide with each other which place you will go and when
